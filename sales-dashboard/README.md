# Sales Performance Dashboard
## This Power BI dashboard is used to analyze the sales information at Superstore to determine the overall sales performance, profitably, and customer contribution. The report allows users to find out the tendencies over time, compare the regions and categories, and the best-performing products.

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Sales by Region

## Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest sales but not the highest profit margin
- The west region contributes the largest share of total revenue
- Sales show a steady upward trend toward year-end.

## Tools used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

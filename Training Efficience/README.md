# Training-Efficience
## This was a training assignment on Data Collection with Kobo and Power BI.

- For this project, I identified an objective and designed a data collection tool using KoBoToolbox. The collected data was used to develop a Power BI dashboard as part of the final assignment.
  KoBoToolbox was connected to Power BI to enable the dashboard to automatically update as new data is submitted.
- The objetive of this survey is to gather feedback from employees on the recent training sessions conducted by the organization.
  The results will help assess the effectiveness and relevance of the training sessions conducted and areas that need improvement. The results of the survey will be presented in a   Power BI dashboard.
